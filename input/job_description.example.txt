Job Title: Marketing Coordinator

Company Overview:
Our company is a rapidly growing e-commerce platform that specializes in selling high-end home decor products. We are seeking a talented Marketing Coordinator to join our team and help us expand our brand awareness and customer reach.

Job Overview:
The Marketing Coordinator will be responsible for supporting the Marketing team in executing marketing strategies, tracking and analyzing data, and managing day-to-day tasks. This is an entry-level position that requires a creative, detail-oriented individual with strong communication and organizational skills.

Key Responsibilities:

Assist in the development and execution of marketing campaigns across multiple channels, including email, social media, and advertising.
Collaborate with the Marketing team to create engaging content, graphics, and visuals for various platforms.
Analyze and report on campaign performance and provide insights for optimization.
Manage the company's social media presence, including scheduling and publishing posts, monitoring engagement, and responding to comments.
Conduct market research and keep up-to-date with industry trends.
Assist with event planning and execution, including trade shows, product launches, and customer appreciation events.
Work cross-functionally with other teams, including Product Development and Customer Service, to ensure cohesive brand messaging and customer experience.
Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field.
1-2 years of experience in a marketing role, preferably in an e-commerce or retail environment.
Strong verbal and written communication skills.
Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and Adobe Creative Suite.
Knowledge of social media platforms and analytics tools.
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple projects simultaneously.
Benefits:

Competitive salary and benefits package.
Opportunity for growth and advancement within the company.
Collaborative and inclusive company culture.
Employee discounts on our products.
If you are a creative, detail-oriented individual with a passion for marketing and a desire to work in a fast-paced environment, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.
